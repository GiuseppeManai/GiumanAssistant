# AI Strategy

AI is becoming the operating system of the enterprise.

## Key Layers
1. **Data Layer**
   - Harmonized, governed data
   - Customer 360 / enterprise data platforms

2. **Intelligence Layer**
   - Models (ML, GenAI)
   - Feature extraction
   - Decision systems

3. **Agentic Layer**
   - Autonomous agents executing tasks
   - Workflow orchestration
   - Human-in-the-loop systems

4. **Action Layer**
   - Integration with enterprise systems
   - APIs, automation, robotics

5. **Governance Layer**
   - Security, compliance, safety
   - Cost control (FinOps)
   - Observability

## Key Trends
- Shift from copilots → autonomous agents
- Flattening of organizations due to AI
- Outcome-based delivery models
- Integration of digital and physical systems

## Execution Model
- Start from high-value use cases
- Rapid prototyping (PoC)
- Pilot at scale
- Industrialize

## Focus
Move from experimentation → production-grade AI systems
